Privacy Policy for SonicRx

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

SonicRx ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Data We Access

SonicRx accesses your Spotify listening data through Spotify's API, including:

  • Your top tracks and artists
  • Your listening history
  • Audio features of tracks you listen to

How We Use Your Data

We use your Spotify data solely to:

  • Generate your weekly and monthly music reports
  • Display your music statistics
  • Create shareable images of your music insights
  • Power home screen widgets

Data Storage

Important: Your data stays on your device.

We store all your music data locally on your iPhone. We do not:

  • Upload your data to our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Sell your information

Third-Party Services

SonicRx uses:

  • Spotify API: To access your listening data. See Spotify's Privacy Policy
  • iCloud: For optional data sync between your devices (if you enable iCloud sync)

Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke Spotify access at any time in your Spotify account settings
  • Delete all app data by deleting the app
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

SonicRx is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
